# EDA Summary Template

This is a short shared template for summarizing EDA work in the Pokemon battle predictor project. We collected battle data from about 13,000 Pokemon random battles, including team composition, Pokemon stats, leading Pokemon, player Elo, in-battle actions, battle duration, switch behavior, and other battle-level features.

The purpose of the EDA is to clean and organize the battle data, understand the structure of the dataset, identify possible sources of bias or leakage, and explore which features may be useful for predicting battle outcomes. These summaries should help connect individual EDA notebooks to the larger modeling goal: estimating which player is more likely to win based on pre-battle and in-battle information.

Each section below briefly describes the research question, the features and methods used, and the main findings.
